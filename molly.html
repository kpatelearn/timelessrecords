Molly Devine is a vibrant singer, songwriter, and DJ known for her soulful voice, heartfelt lyrics, and dynamic live performances. Based in Aotearoa New Zealand, Molly’s music blends rich emotional depth with uplifting energy, capturing both the joy and vulnerability of life.

With a Master’s in Music Performance and years of experience as a recording artist and performer, Molly has toured nationally, supporting legendary acts like Anastacia, The Doobie Brothers, Creedence Clearwater Revival, and Billy Idol. She’s also performed alongside New Zealand music icons Martin Phillips (The Chills), Shayne Carter (Straitjacket Fits), Graeme Downes (The Verlaines), Nadia Reid, and Anthonie Tonnon in the celebrated Dunedin Sound production, Tally Ho! with the Dunedin Symphony Orchestra.

Molly’s latest single, To Be, dives into the raw tension between striving for success and embracing self-acceptance, exploding into a euphoric chorus of self-love and freedom. She’s now gearing up to release Good Vibes Only, a chill, soulful anthem about moving on from heartbreak into a brighter, lighter state of being.

Molly has collaborated with acclaimed producers Greg Haver, Nik Brinkman, and Will Henderson, while also embracing her skills as a self-producer, crafting music that resonates with authenticity and warmth.

Off-stage, Molly is a thoughtful, passionate creative who brings warmth and authenticity to everything she does. She brings warmth, positivity, and a touch of sparkle wherever she goes — and yes, she’s probably singing while she does it.
