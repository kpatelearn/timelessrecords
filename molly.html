About Molly

Molly Devine is a vibrant singer, songwriter, and DJ known for her electrifying live performances and soulful recordings. With a unique blend of disco-soul and pop, she captivates audiences around the globe with her dynamic energy and heartfelt lyrics. Her artistry is a journey of passion and creativity, always pushing the boundaries of musical expression.

“There’s so much space in my heart, I point my life at the stars.”
