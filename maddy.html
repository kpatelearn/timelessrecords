Maddy Low is a versatile musician, songwriter and performer. Described as a born performer by many she meets, Maddy has a knack for grabbing attention and pulling an audience in. Based in Wellington and Auckland New Zealand, Maddy brings an undeniable energy and meaningfulness into each song she writes. Described as a free spirited young woman, Maddy blends genres of indie pop/rock and folk, to craft her unique sound.

“I would describe half my music as the feeling when you are laying down looking at the ceiling, just contemplating life, and the other half as the kind that makes a person simply want to dance around in the sunshine.”

While only being 18 years of age, Maddy has a ton of experience under her belt. Since 2018 she has performed frequently at a number of private events, as well as established restaurants. While mainly performing covers, she has been writing songs since intermediate and has a large repertoire waiting to be released.

A frequent busker since the age of 13, her favourite part of performing is the interactions she has with the people who listen. Maddy is always down to get to know people and have a chat.

“When I busk I get to watch people enjoy themselves, I get kids that will come up to me and tell me how much they like my singing, couples that will dance in the middle of the street, and regular people that stop walking for a while just to sing along. These moments are what make it all feel worth it.”
