About Us

Timeless Records is an independent record label based in Aotearoa New Zealand, supporting emerging artists who are crafting their sound and reaching for wider audiences without compromising who they are.

We work with artists primarily in indie-pop and indie-rock, with a focus on those evolving toward more professional, globally resonant sounds. Our first release, a soul-infused pop track, reflects the kind of genre-blending creativity we celebrate—music that’s grounded in authenticity, but unafraid to grow into something bigger.

We're especially interested in artists transitioning from niche scenes into more widely accessible and globally appealing sounds—without losing what makes them unique.

Artist First, Always

We believe in fair contracts, transparent communication, and sustainable, long-term thinking. Our priority is helping artists build meaningful careers—careers shaped by their own creative vision and supported by a label that listens, cares, and collaborates.

A Forward-Thinking Approach

Timeless Records is digital-first, using modern tools and platforms to help artists connect with their audience, expand their reach, and grow their careers sustainably. We explore innovations like blockchain and emerging monetization models where they serve the artist—but we stay grounded in our core commitment: authentic human music.

Every track we release is created by real people, with real stories and real craft behind it.

Looking Ahead

Our aim is to create a home for artists who are ready to level up—whether through better branding, smarter digital strategies, or new opportunities in sync licensing and cross-media storytelling. We believe in community over competition, creative freedom over formulas, and timeless songs over fleeting trends.

If that resonates with you—whether you’re an artist, a collaborator, or a listener—welcome. We’re just getting started, and we’d love you to be part of the journey.
